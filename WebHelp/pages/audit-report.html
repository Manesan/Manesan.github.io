Audit Report

Description:

This is the Audit Report. This report displays a table with organisations audits (Total Sales, Average Sale Price, Highest Sale Price, Lowest Sale Price, Total Rentals, Average Rental Price, Highest Rental Price, Lowest Rental Price and Total Properties). The report has the organisation details, the report date and the details of the person who prepared the report.
