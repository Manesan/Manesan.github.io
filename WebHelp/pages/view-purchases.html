View purchases

 

Description:

This is the Purchases screen displays all the purchase offers and sale agreements that a client has. The user can search for a specific purchase offer or sale agreement on this screen.

 

 

Reference

Label titled “Purchase Offers”

Card content

A button that opens the “View Purchase Offer” modal to view the specific client’s rental application details when clicked.
